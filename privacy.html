Privacy Policy

VoiceTasks: AI Voice Notes · Last updated: June 20, 2026

VoiceTasks (“we”, “our”, the “App”) is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles and why. We do not require an account, and we do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Information the App handles

Voice recordings & transcripts. When you create a note, the audio you record is sent securely over HTTPS to Google’s Gemini API to transcribe it and turn it into structured tasks. The audio is processed in real time and is not stored on any server we control. The resulting notes (transcript and tasks) are stored locally on your device only.

No account data. The App has no login or account system. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, or location.

Subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives your purchase transaction information and an anonymous, app-generated identifier (not linked to your identity) to validate and restore your subscription.

Notifications. Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. We do not send push notifications and do not collect a push token.

How information is used

  • To transcribe your voice notes and generate structured tasks, summaries, and reminders.
  • To manage and restore your subscription.
  • To operate and improve core app functionality.

Third parties

  • Google (Gemini API) — processes your audio to produce a transcript and structured output. See Google’s privacy terms.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription entitlements.
  • Apple — processes payments and hosts the App.

Data retention & your choices

Your notes live on your device. You can delete any note at any time, or clear all history from Settings → Clear History. Deleting the App removes all locally stored notes.

Security

All network requests use encrypted HTTPS connections.

Children

The App is rated 4+ and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Tracking

The App does not track you across other apps or websites and does not use third-party advertising.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
