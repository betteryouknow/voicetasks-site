Frequently asked

How do I create a note? Tap the microphone on the home screen, speak naturally, then stop. The AI turns your words into structured tasks.

Why does it need my microphone? To record your voice notes. Audio is sent to Google’s Gemini API to transcribe and organize it, and is not stored on a server.

How do reminders work? Tap the bell on any task to schedule a local notification. You’ll be asked for notification permission the first time.

How do I manage my subscription? Settings → Manage Subscription, or your Apple ID → Subscriptions. A 3-day free trial is available for eligible new users; cancel anytime before it ends to avoid charges.

Where are my notes stored? Locally on your device. Clear them anytime from Settings → Clear History.
